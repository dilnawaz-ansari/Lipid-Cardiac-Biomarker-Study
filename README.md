# Lipid Profile & Cardiac Biomarkers Study

# Publication
Journal: International Journal of Pharmaceutical Sciences
DOI: https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.14282423
Published: December 5, 2024
Pages: 543-552, Vol 2, Issue 12

## Study Overview
Cross-sectional study evaluating lipid profiles 
and cardiac biomarkers in 200 cardiac patients
at MGM Hospital, Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar.

## Key Findings
- Total Cholesterol: 325 mg/dl in cardiac patients
  vs 177-211 mg/dl in controls
- LDL-C: 240+ mg/dl across all age groups
- SGOT/SGPT elevated 3x above normal
- Significant CAD-hepatic dysfunction comorbidity
